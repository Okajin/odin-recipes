Lasagna

Here's the recipe of one of the best lasagna in the world. If you like pasta, tomato sauce and a lot of cheese, you're in the right place. Don't worry, follow the recipe, we have your back covered!

Before we get started, please notice that the cook time is long. It's a dish you'll have to be patient to earn it but you won't be disappointed!

Ingredients

  • Meat or vegetarian equivalent
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes and cans of tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Lot of cheeses
  • Egg
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
